Welcome


  • Admissions to the course are made through an All India Entrance Test - Joint Admission Test for M.Sc. (JAM) .
  • Apart from its regular coursework, the course includes Industrial Training of eight weeks and six months of Project Work/Dissertation in various reputed R&D Organizations and industries.

    Information You Need


  • First three semester you will learn about basic Physics.
  • In third semester you have to choose one specialisation (Photonics, Electronics, Instrumentation)
  • Next two semester( 4th and 5th )you pursue in your specialisation and in last semester( 6th )you work on your project.

    Tests & Assignments


  • Every semester for each subject you need to give Minor-1 , Mid-Exams , Minor-2 , End-Exams and Lab Exams .
  • For even semester you also need to give a seminar based upon your topic.
